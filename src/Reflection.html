Keyboard controls:

  • r - Rotate 30 degrees (Denoted "R" above)
  • R - Rotate -30 degrees (R')
  • t - Translate .3 along x-axis (T)
  • T - Translate -.3 along x-axis (T')
  • s - Scale 2x along y-axis (S)
  • S - Scale .5x along y-axis (S')
  • x - Reflect across x axis
  • l - Reflect across line y = mx + b (BXB')
  • o - Reset to identity
The notation "A" represents a translation to the figure's current centroid, A' is its inverse. The notation "B" represents a transform that aligns the line y = mx + b with the x-axis.
